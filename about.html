The heart of every card

The little things mean everything

Tiny Details LLC was founded on the idea that it's the little things that matter most. Each design begins as a hand-drawn colored pencil sketch, full of warmth and character.

When the image in my head and what appears on paper don't quite align, modern AI tools help me refine the details—bringing every card closer to the original idea behind it.

From baby announcements to sympathy cards, every piece is made to celebrate life's moments—big, small, and everything in between.
